OECD Jobble

Welcome to the MVP of OECD Jobble, a GILCAP Angels funded gaming product!

Jobble is a guessing game, in which you guess the country based on the types of jobs people in the country work! With each guess, we'll tell you if you get closer or further, and the distance to the correct country! What fun, oh what fun, to be had by you!

We use real OECD data to build your game world!

The Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) is an intergovernmental organisation with 38 member countries, founded in 1961 to stimulate economic progress and world trade.

The Technology

Inspired by Tradle, OECD Jobble is a full stack gaming experience:

  • Front end in HTML / CSS and Javascript
  • Back end in Golang (coming soon!)
  • Database in SQL (coming soon)
